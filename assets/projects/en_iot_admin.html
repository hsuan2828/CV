【Position each platform for use cases】

While we provide facial recognition cameras, digital signage, food ordering machines, POS machines, temperature and humidity sensors, etc. , for retail customers, they leave tracks of interactions when they come to contact with these equipment during various activities.

From the perspective of the industry development, we believe that these devices are not equipment, but to create more value. It could be seen as an innovative transformation for the team.

Establishing management software platform for each device, we also hope to achieve the effect of product cross-sale and promote services to users.

【What is the Supply Chain and the Demand Exists?】

Generally speaking, we sell these products through SI manufacturers, and SI manufacturers sell these products, while helping installations and troubleshoot for the client.

The customer may be a large catering organization such as WangPin, which has several sub-brands. Under each brand, there will be store administrators or supervisors, and store personnel who will perform their duties.

Therefore, it can be seen that for the data presented what each user wants to see could be different; that is, how to manage permissions is an important issue while designing platforms.

In addition, the data viewed on the mobile device will also should be adjusted accordingly (for example, simplified into necessary data or configuration).

For example, the equipment installation App aims to be designed for installers is to increase the vertical connections and real-time data while having a better grasp of the end-user usage status.

【Start with Management of Services Subscriptions】

In various scenarios, we design a subscription management platform for SI vendors; for SI to enable services and devices with the serial numbers, which could be seen as a method to control selling of integration services.

Users could also check services status/active periods of each brand. And this all comes to our hope that we can continue to have a relationship with users through subscription of software services.

Other issues I am thinking about is also how to make a purchase as easy as possible while buying serial number is a tradition of the electronics industry.

The purchase of serial number requires pre-selection of the service and actual action with sellers. In comparison, whether it is an instant/regular online deduction for storing value across services/devices is something that is resolving.

【Start with the Management of the Stores and Equipment Under the Brand】

General front line managers (such as the general manager of the store) could see the stores, its equipment and overall users of their own brand for management.

【Continuously Iterate the Product after Received Consumer Feedbacks】

As our product used by multiple retailers, we realized we should find a easier way to add on and manage stores. Therefore, in several iterations (1.9.0), the use of excel import function is added, that is, an excel template is provided for data specification setting; in addition, the platform will generate group IDs for groups that have been set on the platform.

Administrators can fill in the familiar excel to facilitate the compilation of a large amount of data permissions, and upload it after completion.

In the operation of the platform itself, the concept of “group” is designed. That is, grouping operations can be done on the platform, allowing managers to compose “groups” arbitrarily to classify a large number of stores and user information.

Take a “store group” as an example. Users can create “groups” and assign permissions for multiple people, so that when consumers create a new single store, they can quickly bring in the authority relationship pre-identified through the “group”.

In addition, it is also allow grouping departments first through “department”. When creating a new single user, you can also bring the permission of the store to the department through the department method.

However, this function, after feedback discussions from actual users, it is easy to be confused with the store group function, so that it has been enabled in the (1.9.1) version.

【Sub-Service Design- Take Temperature and Humidity Monitoring as an Example】

Temperature and Humidity Monitoring has been easy to understand from internal promotion because the business concept is relatively easy to understand.

With the severe epidemic and the rising awareness of food safety, temperature and humidity sensing is required in a variety of scenarios, including exhibition venues, agricultural planting, logistics, retail refrigerators, drug freezers or special wards, etc.

However, it still faces many challenges in the software market, such as hardware sensing capabilities, and should be combined with monitoring and control.

In temperature and humidity monitoring architecture, the data collected by sensors will be and transmitted to the gateway and then sent to the cloud.

And the most important thing is to solve the current problems encountered in the traditional use of thermometers with manual measurement and paper recording methods, including labor costs, personnel accounting records, etc., and through the entire temperature and humidity monitoring solution, it can be more remote in real time.

In terms of UX considerations for huge reports, there are two:

  • To see the sensing result data.
  • See important results intuitively. Find information quickly (including existing report import integration, classification label setting and management). In this process, we went from cooperating with a large-scale merchandise store project to in-depth understanding of customer needs, to summarizing and sorting out the production of standardized products.

At present, most of our products are already sold on the market, and we are constantly receiving feedback from more than 30 customers and thousands of stores.

Moreover, we even established the a beverage store test as a POC site.

The directions of development include:

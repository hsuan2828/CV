Medical Rehabilitation Platform

Good helper for home and community rehabilitation

As a clinical therapist, one of the most common problems happen during clinical practices is the inability to track patient's condition after returning home.

While patients usually get home programs as finishing clinical practices, and some patients even have therapists to conduct home assessments or having a home therapist, this is actually hard to bring the clinical practices continuously back to home. It is also hard for therapist to know whether patients do actual implementation of home program or not.

For some researches; for those patients who are not aware of their home rehabilitation that caused the return to the clinic for more than 2-3 years which causing medical burden.

Thus, as a clinical practitioner, I have been thinking about how to make treatment activities more productive and efficient (how to reduce efforts in repetitive and low-risk activities), or how to make treatment activities more connected by utilizing collected data that could be even utilized in home program. Also, as the epidemic raged, most people cannot meet with partitioners, online solutions of how to make it easier for people to access medical activities and knowledge, and whether home rehabilitation workable through online solution are issues for many patients and the public.

【Market Analysis】

As we target digital solutions of rehabilitation, there are related competitors such as Zhifubao, Dragon Bone King, and Magic Mirror on the market; and also for fitness apps, Switch and Uniigym are already very successful, which makes the competition is fierce.

As most of our team members come with medical backgrounds, we could enhance our competitiveness in the following directions:

  1. The conversion of rehabilitation activities between those utilizing IoT equipment.
  2. The way of transformation of the know-how of quantitative data that leads to rehabilitation suggestions.
  3. The process of quantifiable accumulation for rehabilitation data to assists professional judgments.

【Product Position & Value】

After analyzing the market, we see our product as:

  1. Patients are able to get feedbacks through the platform, reduce the risk of rehabilitation and improve benefits.
    • The patient perceives and corrects his actions.
    • Keep the interaction with the rehabilitation practitioner accordingly accept and adjust rehabilitation tasks.
  2. Make patients feel the companionship in rehabilitation, reduce the dullness of rehabilitation and increase motivation by online social connections.
    • Establish an context of group supervision and find people who can share experience and practice together.

【User Scenarios】

One-stop service is provided from diagnosis, rehabilitation in the hospital, and rehabilitation at home.

The use of rehabilitation products has developed into a wearable device, accumulating health status, and concatenating the rehabilitation information in the hospital with the home rehabilitation and thereby enhancing the continuity of rehabilitation.

【Product Design - Hardware Structure】

It is designed as G-sensors to detect the user's movements while fixing the sensor in the loop and wear it on the limbs.

The sensor uses a nine-axis system to get feedbacks in the form of sound and light; it uses a wired connection with a computer to detect acceleration peaks to analyze the frequency and intensity of actions, and then transmit the data to the cloud via Bluetooth.

【Product Design - Software, Starting from the Effectiveness of the Community】

In software design, how to upgrade tasks and use community methods to promote usage motivation are the key points.

  1. In the task design, the user can select the menu at a glance. Video chat rooms are provided to increase the fun of interaction.

  2. In terms of the feature of community room, while maintaining a certain degree of privacy, user could still find users of the same interests.

【Medical Regulation- whether the Product Should Registered as a Medical Device】

When making medical-related products, the consideration in implementation is whether to register as medical device and which classes of medical device should be classified. The cost, timeline and promotion plans are based on this decision.

After evaluation, we decided not to register it as a medical device while there is no capital to cover the initial investment of medical materials; and we hope that it can be an affordable price that communities can attract a large number of users first.

【Patent - is there Contains Technical or Business Ideas Special Enough to Apply for a Patent?】

Another point of consideration is whether patents’ applications have the power to protect our products and innovative standpoints.

In this product, the possible application directions include-

  1. Real-time multi-sensing device action display (simultaneous display of multi-person actions = community track)
  2. Sound feedback from wearable devices.

After we discussed the product with the patent attorney, one of the direction he suggested is that we should magnify the AI application for clinics.

【How to Make a Profit?】

Since therapists are the professionals that have close relationship with patients, it is more persuasive to promote the products through medical staffs.

While the advantage of our product is to utilize software platform effect to aggrate numbers of users, it could be seen as an integration method with existing tech company in the industry (such as Compal) as a supplement of SASS solutions.

The segmentation of pricing are designed as- while we provide low-priced sensors, the services are rated by subscription (individual rooms are free, and public rooms are semi-gamified)

【Summary - Physical and Online POCs are in Progress】

At present, this project is undergoing patient trials of the product and has indeed received a positive response. We also visited in the stroke mutual aid community, and found that the Health Bureau also echoes that unnecessary treatments should not be admitted to medical institutions and encourages clients of doing home rehabilitation.

Therefore, the team’s questionnaire is used to collect feedback and adjust the product (100 cases in Taipei Veterans General Hospital).

It is expected that this product will continue to be developed and discussed while we are looking for resources of new ventures, competitions, and partners.

Transfer Robot

Cross-platform financial products, using API to allow users to conveniently transfer funds on cooperative platforms.

【As we see the pain points & needs from the market, how could we create value and what is the solution?】

In the process of business development in Fintech market (ig. Fintech Space), we found out there is a strong need of most new entrepreneurs- that is they hope to have account deduction and fund transfer.

The situation is that new entrepreneurs want a low-cost way to allow users to pay online even linked to a designated account on the platform, in scenarios such as online payments to securities dealers, P2P loan payments, and so on.

There are currently two similar products:

  • One is called Account Link- After the user bounds deposit account, the user can transfer the fund from the account to the platform to store value, and then transfer the value from the stored value account on this platform to the designated address when spending. However, this shortcoming is that the entering threshold is high, and the platform itself needs to be an e-payment provider (which needs to prepare 200 million capital and related materials for application to get the e-payment business license).
  • The other is - credit card online deductions. After talking to the bank about this business, the user could key in the card number on the Internet and uses credit cards to make purchases; this service requires a 2% handling fee for each transaction, while it sometimes needs to be reviewed by banks who is willing to undertake the service.

In the interaction with new entrepreneurs, we found that most new entrepreneurs hope to provide cash flow services on the platform, but they are not satisfied with the credit card deduction service because it sometimes fails to meet the standards of the acquiring bank; also, the 2% handling fee is a burden.

For account deductions, it is even harder to run an e-payment service to provide a place for fund storage. Put it simply, there are no services suitable for new entrepreneurs in Taiwan's market for wire transfer of funds.

【How to Meet This Demand? Survey Other Markets? Do Existing Accounts/Credit Cards Have the Opportunity to Break Through?】

So we went to study similar fund transferring products internationally. In fact, it is not difficult to find that there are services for third-party account deductions at home and abroad. With Lydia in the United Kingdom or Venmo in the United States, it is possible to transfer funds on a third-party platform through binding. Users can apply for membership, bind their Email/mobile phone, and set a password. After each login, they need to enter the password and SMS as two-factors verification; using each bank's open outgoing transfer APIs for bank transfer.

【What are the Motivations and Potential Risks of Allowing Third Parties (New Innovations) to Provide Financial Services?】

With the development of technology, more and more businesses are digitized. Governments of various countries want to use new technological advantages to promote industrial development, especially starting with the UK’s open banking policy to force financial services to “Open” their APIs to third parties to stimulate industry competition and progress.

However, from the perspective of corporates, the financial industry is a highly regulated company that already has a certain amount of information security and risk control. Such financial services are subject to many considerations in a third parties’ platforms, such as whether the third party has the ability to contains data and technically to prevent crackers or systems being destroyed.

Looking back to development of credit cards, the online payments of credit cards has gradually established online payment structure on third-party platforms; compared with credit cards, accounts are directly debited and the risk is higher. Credit cards need to be reviewed and then bound, and credit cards will not be deducted immediately.

【Is There a Chance to Make the Service Cross-Platforms?】

In order to expand the benefits of the cooperation platform, make it more convenient for users to use (including no having to bind separately on each platform) and have the opportunity to expand the customer base of different banks in the future. We plan to establish an independent platform that can be called from each platform and online banking, with page for detailed management and binding platform search.

This feature is facing following opportunities and challenges:

  • 1. Users' experience habits, will they feel comfortable, safe and convenient for enabling transfer services on third-party platforms?
  • 2. Do business have the function of being promoted for such a management interface?
  • 3. If you want to bind other banks for payment, there are still great difficulties. The reason is that other banks are willing to cooperate. This means that the bank-related transfer information will be known to our bank. The willingness to be in a state of competition is low.
  • 4. Others: It is necessary to establish a member management database and backstage.

After our evaluation, we still think that this is necessary to have cross-platform functions, but it is divided into two stages. The first stage is based on various processes before and after the second stage, and the second stage adds functions related to the management page.
